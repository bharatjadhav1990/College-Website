About Our Institution

Rooted in tradition, growing with the future

✦ ॐ ✦

Shri Bhairavnath Vidya Mandir and Junior College, Pabal is a premier educational institution nestled in the heart of Shirur Taluka, Pune District, Maharashtra. Founded under the divine blessings of Shri Bhairavnath, our institution has been a beacon of knowledge and character formation for the youth of this region for over three decades.

We offer a holistic education system that blends the rich cultural heritage of Maharashtra with modern pedagogical methods. Our school caters to students from Class 8 through 10 (SSC Board), while our Junior College offers Science, Arts and Commerce streams for Classes 11 and 12 (HSC Board).

Our vision is to nurture not just academically bright students but well-rounded individuals who are grounded in values, compassionate to society, and ready to face the challenges of a dynamic world.

Dear Students, Parents & Well-Wishers,

It is my privilege and honour to serve as the Principal of Shri Bhairavnath Vidya Mandir and Junior College, Pabal — an institution that has, for decades, been a torchbearer of quality education and noble values in our beloved Shirur Taluka.

Education, to us, is not merely the acquisition of knowledge from textbooks. It is the cultivation of a curious, compassionate, and courageous mind — a mind that can think critically, dream boldly, and act responsibly. At this Vidya Mandir, we believe every child carries within them an infinite potential waiting to be discovered and nurtured.

Our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to create a learning environment that is safe, stimulating, and deeply rooted in our cultural identity. We encourage every student to pursue excellence not just in academics, but in sports, arts, and community service — for these are the true hallmarks of a complete human being.

To the parents who have entrusted your children to us: your faith is our greatest responsibility and our deepest inspiration. Together, let us build a future that makes Pabal proud.

— With Warm Regards & Blessings
